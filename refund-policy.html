Refund and License Policy

This policy is written for XENEON Edge Host public sales. Review it before using it in checkout or customer emails.

Refunds

Customers may request a refund within 14 days of purchase if the app cannot be installed, cannot open the local dashboard, or does not work with their supported Windows setup after reasonable troubleshooting.

Refunds can be declined for abuse, chargeback fraud, unsupported modified builds, or requests made after the refund window.

License

A purchase grants one person a non-transferable license to install and use XENEON Edge Host on their own devices. The app may not be resold, redistributed, rented, sublicensed, or bundled into another paid product without written permission.

Independence

XENEON Edge Host is independent software. It is not an official CORSAIR product and is not endorsed by CORSAIR, Ubiquiti, Philips Hue, OpenWeather, Microsoft, Apple, or other integration providers unless a written agreement says otherwise.

Support

Support starts at the support page. A paid public release should also include a monitored support inbox and a monitored security inbox before checkout goes live.
